FAQ

About Ordering

How can I place an order online? Visit our website and browse our product catalog to place an order directly through our secure checkout system.

How far in advance should I place my order? We recommend placing your order at least 3-5 days in advance to ensure availability and timely delivery.

Can I modify my order after placing it? Yes, you can modify your order up to 24 hours before the scheduled delivery time.

About Delivery

What delivery options do you offer? We offer standard, expedited, and same-day delivery options to meet your needs.

Can I track the status of my order? Yes, you can track your order status by logging into your account on our website.

What if I am not at home? If you're not at home during delivery, we can leave your order with a neighbor or in a safe place of your choosing.

About Products

What ingredients are in your desserts? Our desserts are made with high-quality, keto-friendly ingredients. For specific ingredients, please refer to the product descriptions.

How should I store and enjoy these desserts? Store the desserts in a cool, dry place and follow the specific storage instructions provided with each product.

What if I have an allergy to a certain ingredient? Please let us know if you have any dietary restrictions or allergies, and we will do our best to accommodate your needs.

About Payment and Refunds

What payment methods do you accept? We accept various payment methods including credit cards, PayPal, and more.

How can I request a refund? If you are unsatisfied with your purchase, please contact our customer service to request a refund.

What should I do if there is a payment issue? If you encounter any payment issues, please contact our support team for assistance.
